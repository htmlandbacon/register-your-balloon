'Register your balloon' accessibility exercise

You can use this as a worksheet for recording issues you find in this exercise. Copy and paste the below text into a Word Doc, Google Doc, or on your device's notepad.

Testing checklist

  • Can you access everything by pressing the tab key?
  • Does WAVE find any errors?
  • Does WAVE's colour contrast tab find any errors?
  • Do the patterns follow the GDS best practice
  • How is validation working?

Government Digital Services have recently published how to do a basic accessibility check. If you'd like, you could use these checks as an extension task.

If you're having trouble tabbing, and you're using Firefox or Safari, you might need to enable tabbing.

You might also want to document what the impact would be for a user.
